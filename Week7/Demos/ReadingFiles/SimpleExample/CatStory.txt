The Lost Cat
Mittens wandered too far from home.
He finally found his way back and scratched at the front door.
His owner walked right past him without looking.
Mittens meowed as loudly as he could, but no one answered.
Then he noticed a photo of himself with flowers beside it.
That's when Mittens realized he hadn't been lost at all, 
he had passed away, 
and his owner couldn't see him anymore.
Then he heard his owner calling for him. 
It was from behind. 
Turns out he was at the wrong house. 
He turned around and ran home to his owner.
